What is MalCommands?

MalCommands is a collection of command-lines often associated with cyber threats - whether through direct attribution, offensive tool usage, OS utility abuse or other research means.

How should I use it?

However you want - it is meant to be a broad research tool - some usecases may include the following;

  • Discovering tools or commandlines associated with a specific MITRE Technique or Tactic
  • Cross-referencing commandlines discovered in the course of a forensic investigation.
  • Better understanding the purpose or objective of a specific commandline or tool.
  • Understanding the risk or fidelity of a commandline with respect to detection techniques.
  • Finding additional references for a specific command.
  • Determining known threat-actor attributions for certain types of data.

You can't possibly map all commandline variations!

Correct - there is pretty much a limitless way to obfuscate, encode, encrypt or otherwise hide commandline activity - this tool focuses on the final version as interpreted by the base OS when considering standard utility builds - cyber threats can and do change arguments, formats, etc at will when customizing offensive tools.
